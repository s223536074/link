Wildlife in Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka, known as the "Pearl of the Indian Ocean," is a country blessed with stunning natural scenery as well as an incredible variety of species. At the crossroads of Asia, this island nation is home to a remarkable variety of animal species that flourish in its diverse environments, which range from lush rainforests to barren plains and unspoilt coastlines. This article will examine the diverse array of wildlife that adorns Sri Lanka's coastline, emphasising the need for its preservation and the interesting experiences it provides to both locals and tourists.
